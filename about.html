About Kate

I am a bookkeeping and accounting professional based in the Los Angeles area, working with small businesses and solo professionals both locally and remotely.

My background includes hands‑on experience with day‑to‑day bookkeeping, reconciliations, payroll support, and month‑end reporting, as well as working inside established accounting teams.
